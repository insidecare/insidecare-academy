Why this exists

My WHY is the foundation of everything in this Academy

I grew up knowing I wanted to work in health. Not as a clinician. I was never drawn to the bedside. I was drawn to the system behind the bedside. How it is structured, how it makes decisions, how data moves through it, how it accounts for the harm it sometimes causes, and how it plans for the future.

The problem was that nobody could show me a map of this world. I did my undergraduate in Economics and IT in Ireland and I could not see a path in. I got a scholarship to study Health Data Science at the University of Birmingham and suddenly the analytical skills I had built started to connect to something that mattered.

Since then I have worked as a medical device tester, where I learned what happens when healthcare technology fails before it reaches a patient. I worked as a clinical administrator, where I learned the gap between policy and operational reality. And now I work in Departmental Governance and Coordination, where I sit at the intersection of clinical practice, data, and the frameworks that are meant to protect people.

InsideCare exists because I built the knowledge I needed one role at a time, and I want you to have it before you need it.

"Every role I held felt like a detour at the time. Looking back, each one was a door. This Academy is the map I wish someone had handed me at the beginning."
